lasagna

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make the world's greatest lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let rest for 5-10 mins and enjoy!
